Lifecycle Stage: Usage Phase
Risk Category: User Awareness Risk

Scenario Description:
Consumers treat IoT devices as 'install-and-forget' appliances. There is a disconnect between user expectations and the technical reality of maintenance (passwords/updates) and legal obligations (privacy laws).

Risk Mechanism:
